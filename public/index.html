About Me

I'm a front-end developer seeking a full-time role where I can help an organization achieve their goals.

My journey into web development traces back to my early encounter with a Commodore 64, igniting a lifelong fascination with technology. However, it was Jennifer Robbin's influential web design book that sparked my enthusiasm for crafting digital experiences, and I've been immersed in the world of web development ever since.

Continuously honing my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I specialize in designing visually captivating websites that prioritize seamless user experiences. Recently, I've delved into the realm of static-site generators to enhance site performance and scalability, always seeking new ways to elevate the quality of my work.

Beyond coding, I find inspiration in literature, thought-provoking podcasts, and British crime dramas. These diverse interests enrich my creativity and inform my problem-solving approach, infusing a unique perspective into my development projects.

I'm eager to bring my technical expertise and innovative spirit to a collaborative team dedicated to pushing the boundaries of web development. Let's create something remarkable together!
